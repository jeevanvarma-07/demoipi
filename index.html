Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions
Answered

Find answers to the most common questions about our programs, admission, and online learning experience.

Our online learning platform provides recorded video lectures, reading materials, assignments, and live interactive sessions. Students can access all materials 24/7 from any device with an internet connection. Each course includes mentorship, discussion forums, and practical ministry projects to ensure comprehensive learning.

Absolutely. Our online programs are designed to be accessible to students from any country. We have graduates serving in over 30 nations. All you need is an internet connection and a desire to grow in biblical knowledge and ministry skills.
Requirements vary by program. Generally, certificate programs require basic literacy and a personal testimony of faith. Diploma and degree programs may require previous educational qualifications. A pastoral or ministry recommendation is appreciated but not always mandatory. Contact our admissions office for specific program requirements.
We strive to keep our programs affordable and accessible. Fee details are provided upon application. We also offer scholarships and financial assistance for deserving students, especially those in full-time ministry or from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Contact our admissions team for details.
Program duration varies: Certificate programs are typically 6 months, Diploma programs take about 1 year, the Bachelor of Theology is a 3-year program, and specialized training programs range from 9 to 12 months.
